Our Transparency Promise
🔐
Zero Patient Cash
The person in need never receives funds directly. Every donation is paid directly into the pharmacy's billing system against a specific prescription order. This eliminates fraud, builds donor trust, and protects recipients from liability.
🔗
Every Cent Has a Tracking ID
Each transaction generates a unique CVS or H-E-B Pharmacy Transaction ID — verifiable independently through the pharmacy's customer support or digital portal. Donors can confirm their money reached the medication at any time, forever.
🌊
Overflow Benefits Everyone
When a campaign is fully funded, overflow doesn't disappear — it flows into the Community Pool, a transparent reserve deployed to the next verified urgent case. The pool balance is public, real-time, and auditable.
